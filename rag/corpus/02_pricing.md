# Aster Cloud Storage — Pricing

## Storage Pricing (per GB / month)

| Storage class | Price |
|---|---|
| Standard | $0.023 |
| Infrequent Access | $0.0125 |
| Archive | $0.0036 |

## Request Pricing

PUT, COPY, POST, LIST requests cost $0.005 per 1,000 requests. GET and all other requests cost $0.0004 per 1,000 requests.

## Data Transfer

The first 100 GB of data transferred out to the internet each month is free. After that, transfer is billed at $0.09 per GB for the first 10 TB, with volume discounts above that tier. Data transferred between Aster and the customer's compute instances in the same region is free.

## Free Tier

New accounts receive 5 GB of Standard storage, 20,000 GET requests, and 2,000 PUT requests free for the first 12 months.

## Billing Cycle

Bills are calculated daily and invoiced monthly in arrears. There is no minimum monthly commitment.
